About Airsell Cargo

Airsell Cargo is a professional, licensed freight forwarding and logistics company. We specialize in multimodal transportation including air cargo, sea freight, customs clearance, and supply chain management. Positioned strategically in Hargeisa with access to Hargeisa Airport and Berbera Port, we facilitate seamless trade connections across East Africa and beyond. With adherence to international standards (IATA, FIATA, IATA ONE Record), we deliver transparent, compliant, and efficient logistics solutions.
