Airsell Cargo | About Us

Airsell Cargo is a premier multimodal logistics and freight forwarding agent headquartered in Hargeisa, Somaliland. Founded in 2024, we specialize in bridging the gap between local markets and global trade hubs through Hargeisa Airport and Berbera Port. As a forward-thinking, non-IATA agent, we are dedicated to professionalizing the logistics industry by integrating digital manifest systems and cloud-based tracking to ensure seamless, transparent, and secure cargo movement.

Core Services

We provide comprehensive end-to-end logistics support, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination efficiently.

Air Freight Forwarding

Expert handling and expedited air transport via Hargeisa Airport.

Sea Freight Forwarding

Reliable ocean freight and cargo consolidation through Berbera Port.

Multimodal Logistics

Seamless integration of transport modes for optimized global delivery.
