Our Story & Mission

Who We Are

Founded in 2024, Airsell Cargo is a leading freight forwarding agent based in Hargeisa. We bridge the gap between local producers and global markets by providing specialized logistics for air and sea freight.

We are not just a transport company; we are a digital-first logistics partner. Our team is dedicated to moving Somaliland’s exports—from livestock to frankincense—with the highest standards of documentation and speed.

Our Mission

To empower Somaliland’s economy by providing transparent, fast, and automated logistics solutions that connect local businesses to the world.
