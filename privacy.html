Privacy Policy

At Airsell Cargo, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with our website and services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details submitted via forms (name, email, phone).
  • Shipment and service inquiries.
  • Affiliate marketing referral data (if applicable).
  • Technical data such as cookies and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide logistics services.
  • Improve our website and client communication.
  • Track affiliate referrals and ensure proper crediting.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except when required by law or to fulfill service obligations. Affiliate tracking data may be shared with partners strictly for commission purposes.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience and track affiliate referrals. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us directly.
